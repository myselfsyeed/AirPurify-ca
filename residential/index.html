Frequently Asked Questions

How do UV light systems work in purifying air?

UV light systems use ultraviolet light to inactivate airborne pathogens and microorganisms like mold, bacteria, and viruses. This technology disrupts the DNA of these contaminants, preventing them from multiplying and causing harm.

Are UV light systems safe for residential use?

Absolutely! Residential UV light systems are designed with safety in mind. They are installed within the HVAC system or other locations where there is no direct exposure to occupants, ensuring a safe and healthier living environment.

How often does the UV light system require maintenance?

UV light systems typically require minimal maintenance. However, it is recommended to have the system checked annually and the UV bulb replaced approximately once a year to ensure optimal performance.

Can UV light systems eliminate odors?

Yes, UV light systems are effective in eliminating unpleasant odors. They neutralize odors at the source, leaving your living space smelling fresh and clean.

What are VOCs, and how does UV light destroy them?

VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds) are harmful chemicals emitted by certain solids and liquids, such as paints and cleaning supplies. UV light systems break down these compounds at a molecular level, neutralizing their harmful effects and purifying the air.

Can I install the UV light system myself?

While some homeowners may opt for DIY installation, we recommend professional installation to ensure the system is correctly fitted, positioned, and functioning properly for optimal air purification.

AccordWill a UV light system increase my energy bills?ion Title

UV light systems are energy-efficient and consume minimal electricity. Any increase in your energy bill should be negligible, especially when considering the benefits of improved air quality and health.

How long does it take to notice the difference in air quality after installation?

Many users report noticing improvements in air quality within a few days to a couple of weeks, depending on the initial air quality and the specific conditions of the home.

Can I use UV light systems in conjunction with HEPA filters?

Absolutely! Combining UV light systems with HEPA filters can offer comprehensive air purification, addressing both particulate matter and microbial contaminants for enhanced indoor air quality.
